[Release] wallabag 2.0.1

Date Mon 11 April 2016 Tags v2 / release Share Diaspora* ❄ Twitter ❄ Facebook ❄ Email

It's time to update! We released wallabag 2.0.0 a week ago and it was a success :

  • more than 650 downloads for the package
  • our tweet was retweeted more than 50 times.
  • we've got two articles on French big websites: Numerama and NextInpact

And some issues too! That's why we release wallabag 2.0.1.
