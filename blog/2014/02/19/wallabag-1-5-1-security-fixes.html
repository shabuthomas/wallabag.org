  • Fix: security problem with tags, thank you @mariroz

  • Add: duplicate check added when you save a new article, thank you @mariroz

Download wallabag 1.5.1 here.

To update your wallabag, you have to extract the archive in your folder, delete all files in /cache and delete install folder.

wallabag is free (as in freedom) and opensource. It's made by Nicolas Lœuillet, Thomas Citharel, Jérémy Benoist and many great contributors.

This website is powered by Pelican and pelican-bootstrap3. You can contribute to it on github.com/wallabag/wallabag.org.
